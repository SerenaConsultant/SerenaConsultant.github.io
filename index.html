About Me

As a People and Culture Management & Analyst Professional and People Data Analyst with over 4 years of experience, I specialize in recruitment and organizational psychology.

I have solid expertise in mid- and senior-level hiring across both domestic and international markets, with hands-on experience collaborating with clients in the USA, UK, and Europe.

By integrating the principles of organizational psychology, I help enhance organizational strategies, team dynamics, and behavioral assessments to optimize talent acquisition.

I am dedicated to identifying the right talent and aligning candidates seamlessly with organizational goals through strategic workforce planning and data-driven recruitment solutions.

Through the development of position-specific case studies and the implementation of in-depth fit analyses, I ensure that the recruitment process is not only efficient and insight-driven but also delivers high-quality and lasting placements.
