About

I help organizations align their people strategies with their business goals. With a focus on strategic recruitment, talent acquisition, and organizational analysis, I partner with companies to build effective, human-centered solutions that foster both individual and collective growth.

Services

Strategic Recruitment and Talent Acquisition Consulting

Designing and managing competency-based recruitment processes specifically for mid and senior-level positions. Identifying and placing the most suitable candidates through case studies and behavioral assessment methods.

Recruitment Process Management

Planning and conducting the entire recruitment process professionally, including job posting, application screening, interviews, reference checks, and offer stages.

Performance Management

Establishing systems to measure individual performance by analyzing employees’ strengths and areas for development. Also, developing strategies to increase motivation and satisfaction.

Behavioral Assessment and Psychometric Tests

Conducting personality and competency measurements using scientific tests to analyze job fit.

Organizational Structure and Culture Development

Analyzing internal structures to build more effective team dynamics and improve interdepartmental communication.

Exit Interviews and Employee Experience Analysis

Managing and reporting on employee offboarding processes by analyzing feedback from departing employees.

Case Study Development and Evaluation

Analyzing candidates’ problem-solving skills and role fit with position-specific case studies to select the most suitable candidates in line with the company’s culture and goals.

HR Analytics and Reporting

Providing strategic data to management with candidate evaluation reports, performance analyses, and organizational efficiency reports. Collecting organizational data and conducting in-depth analyses on metrics such as workforce performance, employee satisfaction, and interdepartmental collaboration. Evaluating employee and manager productivity, motivation, and engagement.
