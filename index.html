About

I help organizations align their people strategies with their business goals. With a focus on strategic recruitment, talent acquisition, and organizational analysis, I partner with companies to build effective, human-centered solutions that foster both individual and collective growth.

Services

Strategic Recruitment & Talent Acquisition

Designing and managing competency-based recruitment processes specifically for mid and senior-level positions. Identifying and placing the most suitable candidates through case studies and behavioral assessment methods.

Recruitment Process Management

Professional management of job postings, application screening, interviews, reference checks, and offer stages.

Performance Management

Establishing systems to evaluate individual performance, analyze strengths, and build strategies to enhance motivation and engagement.

Behavioral & Psychometric Assessments

Measuring personality traits and competencies using scientific tools to evaluate job-role fit accurately.

Organizational Development

Analyzing internal structures to improve team dynamics and cross-department communication.

Exit Interviews & Experience Analysis

Gathering and analyzing insights from exiting employees to improve retention and workplace satisfaction.

Case Study Development

Creating and evaluating position-specific scenarios to test candidate alignment with company goals and culture.

HR Analytics & Reporting

Delivering insights to leadership through candidate evaluation summaries, performance metrics, and organizational health reports.
