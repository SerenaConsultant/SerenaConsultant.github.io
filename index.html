About

As a People & Culture Management and Analysis Consultant, I specialize in designing strategic and human-centered HR practices tailored for mid and senior-level talent needs. With a solid foundation in organizational psychology, I help companies build data-driven and psychologically aligned recruitment systems that enhance team dynamics, increase long-term engagement, and support sustainable growth.

My work integrates behavioral science with recruitment strategy — from psychometric tests to case-based evaluations. Whether establishing new processes or optimizing existing frameworks, I provide consultancy to align people strategy with business goals.

Services

  • Strategic Recruitment & Talent Acquisition: Designing and managing competency-based recruitment processes specifically for mid and senior-level positions.
  • Recruitment Process Management: Professional management of job postings, application screening, interviews, reference checks, and offer stages.
  • Performance Management: Establishing systems to evaluate individual performance, analyze strengths, and build strategies to enhance motivation and engagement.
  • Behavioral & Psychometric Assessments: Measuring personality traits and competencies using scientific tools to evaluate job-role fit accurately.
  • Organizational Development: Analyzing internal structures to improve team dynamics and cross-department communication.
  • Exit Interviews & Experience Analysis: Gathering and analyzing insights from exiting employees to improve retention and workplace satisfaction.
  • Case Study Development: Creating and evaluating position-specific scenarios to test candidate alignment with company goals and culture.
  • HR Analytics & Reporting: Delivering insights to leadership through candidate evaluation summaries, performance metrics, and organizational health reports.
